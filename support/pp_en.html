Privacy Policy

Information Collection And Use

Wingman is developed base on a Software Development Kit(DJI SDK) provided by DJI, Wingman would access your mobile GPS location and DJI device information legitimately to provide valued function. These information only processed by local application. Wingman do not collect and upload any information though the internet to server.

APP Activate

DJI aircraft firmware require mobile applications that control DJI aircraft to be activated with the user's DJI account, if Wingman is being used in China. This will ensure operators use the correct set of geospatial information and flight functions for their aircraft, as determined by their geographical location and user profile. Outside of China, the SDK will automatically activate the application without requiring the user to log in.

If Wingman is not activated, all camera live streams will be disabled, and flight will be limited to a cylinder of 100m diameter and 30m height to ensure the aircraft stays within line of sight.
